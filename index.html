BMR and TDEE Calculators


About the BMR Calculator

Basal Metabolic Rate, BMR, is defined as the rate of energy expenditure per unit time by endothermic animals, such as humans, at rest.

Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basal_metabolic_rate

In this calculator, you can calculate your own BMR by responding to the form below.

What is your weight (lbs)?



What is your height (in)?



What is your age (yrs)?



What is your gender?






About the TDEE Calculator

Total Daily Energy Expenditure, TDEE, is defined as the estimate of the calories one burns in order to function on a day-to-day basis

Source: https://www.manvsweight.com/what-is-tdee-meaning-definition/>

Once you have figured out your BMR, you can calculate your TDEE by responding to the form below.

What is your activity level?






About This Application

This is Edrice Sediq and Fredrik Eriksson's first application. The following inputs are taken from the user: weight, height, age, gender, and activity level. The output thus far is the BMR and TDEE of the user based on the user inputs. The program will also respond appropriately to invalid inputs.
